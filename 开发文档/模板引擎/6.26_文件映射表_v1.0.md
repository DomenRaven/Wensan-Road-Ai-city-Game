## 七、数据流（B6 launch + R3）

```text
edu-wizard.launchGame()
  → EduOrientation.getViewportPayload()
  → POST /sessions/{id}/play/launch
  → godot_launcher.launch(..., layout_rect)
  → subprocess Godot
  → godot_window_layout.place_by_pid(pid, rect)
  → 响应 window_placed
  → renderLaunchStatusPanel 更新文案
```
